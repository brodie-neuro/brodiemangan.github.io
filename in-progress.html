In-Progress

Entries here describe ongoing manuscripts at a high level, with methods and implementation details deliberately withheld during peer review to protect novelty.

  • Mangan, B. E., Kourtis, D. The Missing Link: Bridging Cognitive Fatigue with Working Memory Under review Scope: Conceptual synthesis that unifies working-memory oscillatory dynamics with cognitive fatigue, outlining testable predictions and a measurement framework. Specifics withheld to protect novel theoretical linkage and prediction set.
  • Mangan, B. E., Kourtis, D., & Tomaz, S. A. WAND (Working-memory Adaptive-fatigue with N-back Difficulty): A Method for Inducing Cognitive Fatigue Manuscript in prep Scope: Empirical validation of the WAND protocol, a novel open-source framework for inducing active cognitive fatigue. The study demonstrates the protocol's ability to systematically control for common confounds (e.g., learning effects, monotony) to produce a reliable decline in working memory performance at the behavioural level.
